How to View in AR

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Print the Hiro marker
  3. Point your camera at the printed marker
  4. Move around to view the atom from different angles
  5. Observe electrons orbiting at different speeds
Note: AR experience works best on mobile devices with good lighting conditions
